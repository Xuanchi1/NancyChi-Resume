Emperor elementary school was the first school I attended in the United States. This is one of the public elementary schools in the Temple City district, California. The community around the school is an ideal place to live. I have lived in this safe and diverse community since I moved to America. In the neighborhood, all the people were friendly, and the environment inside the school and around my house was clean and green. The street I lived on was the backstreet of my elementary school. My home was so close to the school that I joined a group of children that walked to school every morning. As every morning children will walk together in this group to go to school. We usually go through the back door to get into school. I still remember the first day I attended this elementary school. My first thought about the school when I walked into it was that the environment was full of happiness. I spent a couple of years attending elementary school in China, which is different from what I see at Emperor Elementary. Back in China, we can hear students readinh out loud the homework that the teacher assigned from yesterday, when classes did not even start.
